Who We Are

Insight Sphere BD is a premier industrial engineering consultancy and training center, committed to advancing the field of industrial engineering in Bangladesh. Established by a team of passionate industry experts and academicians, we bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Our team consists of experienced professionals who have worked with leading organizations across various sectors, bringing real-world expertise to our courses and consultancy services.

What We Offer

We provide comprehensive training programs, workshops, and certification courses designed to enhance professional skills and knowledge in industrial engineering disciplines. Our offerings include:

  • Specialized courses in lean manufacturing, quality management, and process optimization
  • Professional development workshops for career advancement
  • Customized consultancy services for businesses seeking to improve operational efficiency
  • Industry-recognized certifications to validate expertise
